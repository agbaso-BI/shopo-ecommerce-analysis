# 🛍️ Shopo E-Commerce Sales Analysis — Power BI

A multi-page Power BI dashboard analyzing e-commerce sales performance 
across customers, products, discounts, and shipping operations.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop

## 📂 Data Source
Dataset obtained from [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com)

## 💡 Key Insights
- 1M total sales with an average revenue of $1,209 per product
- Decor and Furniture are the top performing sub-categories
- Bank Transfer is the most used payment method at 36.41%
- Houston, TX has the highest shipping cost at $717.81 across 58 orders
- Laptop has the highest shipping cost among all products
- Sales peaked around March 2022 and dropped sharply towards January 2023
